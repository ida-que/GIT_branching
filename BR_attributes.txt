Bug report attributes:
1. Number (ID)
2. Severity
3. Priority
4. Title
5. Environment
6. Precondition (optional)
7. STR (steps to reproduce) - 1,2,3,4....n
8. AR (actual result)
9. ER (expected result)
10. Attachment (link to video)
11. Post condition (optional)
12. Reproducibility (optional) - e.g. 4/10
